## 2024-05-18 - Accessibility focus and hints

**Learning:** Important buttons should have accessible tooltips for disabled states, icon labels, and keyboard-visible focus styles.
**Action:** When creating or modifying critical buttons, ensure they have `focus-visible:ring-4` or similar visible focus styles, include icons with proper alignment, and explain to users why they are disabled (e.g. `title="Wypełnij poprawnie..."`).
